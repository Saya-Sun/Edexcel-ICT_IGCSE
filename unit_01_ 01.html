UNIT 1.1 – DIGITAL DEVICES

Explore modern technology used in everyday life

What are Digital Devices?

Digital devices are electronic devices that process, store and output data. They range from powerful computers to small embedded systems used daily.

Types of Digital Devices

Personal Computers
Mobile Phones
Cameras
Entertainment Systems
Navigation Aids
Smart Home Devices

Personal Computers

Desktops

  • More space for components
  • Easy to upgrade
  • Require peripherals
  • All-in-one options available

Laptops

  • Portable and compact
  • Built-in screen and battery
  • Use anywhere
  • High-performance options

Single-Board Computers

Small, low-cost computers used in education and projects.

Example: Raspberry Pi

📱 Mobile Phones

Basic Mobile Phones
  • Use SIM card
  • Calls and texts only
Specialist Phones
  • For specific users
  • Emergency alert phones
  • Talking phones (visually impaired)
  • Large buttons / hearing support
Smartphones
  • Internet access
  • Apps support
  • Camera, GPS, music
  • Touchscreen
  • Needs frequent charging

📲 Tablets

  • Larger than smartphones
  • Touchscreen devices
  • Apps + internet access
  • Some support SIM cards

📷 Cameras & Camcorders

  • Use sensors and lenses
  • Cameras → photos
  • Camcorders → videos
  • Modern devices do both
Image Quality Depends On:
  • Lens quality
  • Image processor
  • Sensor resolution

🎬 Home Entertainment Systems

Televisions
  • Display video/images
  • Resolution: 720p → 8K
  • Smart TVs run apps
Sound Systems
  • High-quality audio
  • USB, Bluetooth, Wi-Fi
PVR
  • Record TV programs
Blu-ray & DVD
  • Play discs
  • Blu-ray = higher quality

🎮 Game Consoles

  • Play video games
  • Controllers (wired/wireless)
  • Motion sensors / VR
  • Stream music & movies
  • Internet connection
Handheld Consoles:
  • Portable
  • Less powerful
  • Limited storage

📺 Media Players

  • Play video/audio on TVs
  • Stream or local storage
  • Controlled by remote/apps

🧭 Navigation Aids

  • Use GPS
  • Provide routes & alerts
  • Used in vehicles & walking
  • Often replaced by smartphones

🏠 Home Automation

  • Smart home devices
  • Control lighting & temperature
  • Operate via apps/internet

🔗 Convergence

Multifunctional Devices

Devices that perform multiple tasks (e.g., smartphone).

Convergence
  • Devices combine features
  • Smartphone = camera + GPS + music
  • Tablet = laptop features
  • Fewer separate devices needed
