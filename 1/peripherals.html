📌 Overview

Peripheral devices are devices that can be connected to a computer system such as a PC or tablet. They extend the functionality of the computer.

Peripherals can be connected internally or externally and are divided into three main types:

⌨️ Input Devices

Used to enter data into a computer.

🖨️ Output Devices

Used to display or present data.
