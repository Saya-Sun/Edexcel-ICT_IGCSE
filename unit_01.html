📱 Types of Digital Devices

Personal Computers

Desktops, laptops, and single-board computers used for everyday tasks.

Mobile Devices

Smartphones, tablets, and specialist phones with advanced features.

Cameras

Capture photos and videos using digital sensors and lenses.

Entertainment Systems

Televisions, consoles, and media players for audio-visual content.

Navigation Aids

GPS-based devices that help users find routes and locations.

Smart Devices

Home automation and smart assistants controlling home functions.

🔄 Multifunction & Convergence

Modern devices combine multiple functions into one device. For example, smartphones act as cameras, GPS, and gaming devices. This merging of technologies is called convergence.

⚙️ Features of Digital Devices

  • Portability
  • Performance
  • Storage
  • User Interface
  • Connectivity
  • Media Support
  • Energy Consumption
  • Expansion Capability
  • Security Features

🖥️ Peripheral Devices

Input Devices

Keyboard, mouse, scanner, microphone, sensors.

Output Devices

Monitor, printer, speakers, projector.

Storage Devices

HDD, SSD, USB drives, optical disks.

💾 Storage Basics

  • Secondary storage is non-volatile
  • Types: HDD, SSD, Optical, Flash
  • Data stored in binary (0s and 1s)
