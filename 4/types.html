📡 Communication Methods

📊 Table 4.2: Communication Methods
Method Description Uses
Satellite Uses satellites to send signals GPS, TV, military
Broadcast Signals sent to many devices TV and radio
Device-to-device Direct connection Bluetooth, USB
🛰️ Satellite Communication
Satellites send and receive signals using antennas.
  • Global coverage
  • Always available
❌ Blocked by buildings/tunnels ❌ Affected by weather
📍 GPS
  • 24 satellites orbit Earth
  • 4 needed for location
📺 Satellite TV
  • DVB-S standard
  • Uses satellite + set-top box
📡 Broadcast Communication
  • Signals sent from transmitters
  • Received by antenna
📺 Digital TV (DVB-T)
  • Ground transmitters
  • Supports HDTV
📻 Digital Radio (DAB)
  • More stations
  • Shows text info
